# Experiments Summary

This document consolidates the saved experiment reports and machine-readable summaries without rerunning anything.

Conventions:
- `Reference` means a baseline/control row rather than a formal accept/reject decision.
- `Comparison only` means the run was informative but not part of a formal accept/reject gate.
- All reported errors are test-set metrics unless noted otherwise.
- Non-training front-end diagnostics are listed separately at the end; epochs/data are marked `N/A` there.
